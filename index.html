Our Project

Our sailing boats Atlantis and Nikoleta have a maximum capacity of 8 to10 persons, so don’t hesitate to share this once of lifetime experience with your loved ones, friends or extended family. We offer two cruises, both stopping at a selection of some of the best beaches on the island, with a delicious barbeque included in the price.

The boat has a full range of facilities including a bar, two toilets, interior seating, upper sun decks and a sheltered seated area at the rear of the boat. The Eros II operates from late May/early June until the end of September.

FAQ

  • What is included in price?
    • All fuel and taxes, drinks (coffee, wine, juice and soft drinks) are included in the price.
  • Is diving included in cruise too?
    • No, due to Health and Safety precautions we do not provide snorkeling equipment. Everyone is welcome to bring their own, if that is your interest
  • What if we reserve our cruise but it turns out that it's going to be bad weather. What happens then?
    • In the event of your trip being cancelled due to bad weather conditions, we will either re-book your trip on the next available sailing or offer you a full refund.
  • Do you work all year around?
    • We operate from late May/early June until the end of September.
